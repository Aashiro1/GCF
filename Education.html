Support for Little Nuns at Anandakuti Vidyapitha

August 2022

The NIBS Great Compassion Fund, in collaboration with the Maitri Project (www.themaitriproject.com), provided financial support to the 15 young nuns studying at Anandakuti Vidyapitha, Svayambhu. These nuns, who are dedicated to their spiritual and academic education, greatly benefited from this act of generosity. Mr. Sujan Bajracharya (Vajra Sujan), the principal of the Vidyapitha, graciously accepted the donation on their behalf, expressing gratitude for the much-needed support. This contribution will aid the nuns in continuing their studies and pursuing their spiritual path with fewer financial burdens.
